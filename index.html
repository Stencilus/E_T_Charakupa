Research

Research Interests

My research focuses on SME financing, credit access, and the financial challenges businesses face in emerging markets. I am particularly interested in how credit processes, risk perceptions, and financial management practices influence SME growth and loan performance.

Selected Research Work

Effects of Exchange Rate and Interest Rate Changes on Loan Repayments

Overview: This study examines how macroeconomic volatility affects borrowers' repayment capacity in Zimbabwe's banking sector. It analyzes how exchange rate movements and interest rate adjustments influence loan performance and credit risk.

Key Insights:

  • Currency depreciation significantly increases repayment challenges for borrowers.
  • Interest rate shocks disproportionately affect SMEs with limited financial buffers.
  • Stronger financial management practices improve resilience during economic volatility.

Relevance: The findings reinforce the importance of robust credit assessment, SME capacity building, and adaptive risk management in emerging market banking.
